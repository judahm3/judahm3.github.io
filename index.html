the microbit


About the Microbit v1

The micro:bit is a small digital device which you can use to control physical computing projects. Unlike the Crumble microcontroller, you can also use a micro:bit on its own, because it has some built-in inputs and outputs. Like a Crumble, you need to program the micro:bit from another device. You can write simple games, create electronic projects and work your way up to small but complex programs. I've used a Mac, PC and an iPad to program the Micro:bit all very easy to do if you follow the instructions. It's fun and has bags of potential for not a lot of money. Loads of support and example programs online.

Judah's Review:

I agree that the Microbit is an interlocking device that entertains people into coding and programming microcomputers. The v1 has a bunch of small (but very powerful) microchips. It also has a bunch of sensors, such as a light sensor, magenotemeter sensor. I rate it a 7/10.
